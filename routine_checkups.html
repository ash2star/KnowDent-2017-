Why Are Routine Checkups Necessary??

A dentist is the one that recommends how often one should have a routine checkup. Most veople visit their dentist only once or twice a year; whereas some people avoid doing even that due to the cost of a dental checkup or past experience being bad. The dentist examines one's teeth and gums for any type of decay, gum or other problems.
